About Me

Hello & Welcome! My name is Jon, I’m a web developer with a degree in supply chain and a background in search results and landing pages evaluations.

My interest for web building dates back long ago when I learned HTML & CSS on my own. After management changes in my supply chain job, I asked myself what I would like to do most disregard my formal training. So I started my career change as a consultant working to improve the relevancy of search results generated by the search engine Bing and to evaluate landing pages. After a couple of years, thousands of landing pages later. I decided to take a deep dive into coding school for the Web Development Immersive Program. And really find out how sites are put together, what powers the web, & how it all works!

Ultimately, it’s about learning new skills, it’s about finding innovative & better ways of doing things, it’s about building things, and last but not least, it’s about creativity & ingenuity. I’m open-minded, organized, & dedicated to my work. Once I had a last minute bug that I stayed up until I found a solution at 4:30AM and met the deadline the next day. My goal is to build creative apps & websites that benefit the organization & the user, making the web a better place.
